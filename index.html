Frequently Asked Questions

How much will a website cost me?

The cost will largely depend on the complexity and features to be implemented. We are available to discuss your requirements and give you a quote. Once you have placed your order with us, we will adhere to our quoted price.

How long does it take to finish?

Again, the time it takes will depend on the particular requirements and complexity of the project. We pride ourselves in completing our work within the deadlines scoped during our project planning.

Do we need to meet in person and how often?

No. Meeting in person is not necessary. We will use our client's preferred platform of communication and email, and can discuss and review changes. The site is not made public until it works as required and you approve it.

Will people be able to find the site online?

Yes. Our websites are search engine optimized and we will be available to re-evaluate your website to ensure compliance with search engine guidelines.

Will I need any other services that Glidev does not provide?

Unfortunately we cannot help you hire a hitman or bring you coffee to your office. Otherwise, GliDev will handle all your website and application needs from domain registration, feature implementation and maintainance.

What if I don't like the design?

If you do not like the first design, we are available to come up with another design you like until you are completely satisfied.

Can you improve my current website for more business?

Yes. GliDev provides a free website evaluation and show you how it can be improved to reach and retain more customers in your line of business.
